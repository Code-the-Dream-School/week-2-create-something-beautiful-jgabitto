The Beautiful Game: Football

Lionel Messi: The Greatest Ever

Lionel Messi is considered the greatest soccer player of all time. Messi has scored more than 700 goals for both Barcelona and the Argentina national team, has won 34 club trophies, and has six Ballon D'Ors. Aside from the stats, his style of play will take your breath away!

Hernan Crespo: Lethal Goal Poacher

Hernan Crespo is one of the best goal scorers of all time! Crespo has scored 273 goals for club and country and won titles with Chelsea, Inter, A.C. Milan to name a few! My favorite goals of his were the ones during the 2006 Fifa World Cup against Ivory Coast, Serbia and Montenegro, and Mexico. Crespo was my favorite player growing up and he always will be!

Steven Gerrard: Liverpool's Chosen Son

What is there to say about Steven Gerrard. Liverpool born and bred. Made 710 appearances for the Reds and scored 186 goals for them. Scored crucial goals when Liverpool needed it such as the 2004 Champions League Final, 2006 FA Cup Final, and the volley against Olympiacos that prevented Liverpool from being knocked out of the champions league. I've been a huge fan of his since I was 12! YNWA!

Luis Suarez: El Pistolero

Luis Suarez is one of the deadliest and lethal goal scorers the world has ever seen. He has scored at least 50 goals during his time at Ajax, Liverpool FC, FC Barcelona and the Uruguayn national team. What makes Suarez so special isn't just the number of goals he's scored, but the goalazos he's made. He's one of my favorite players of all time!

Juan Sebastian Veron: La Brujita

Juan Sebastian Veron is a magical player that hasn't gotten a lot of credit. His time at Manchester United and Chelsea were less than expected, but his playing style is what sets him a part from everyone else. He's tall, strong, fast, and great with the ball. He is known for his long passes that can find his teammates in any position. I love Veron!
